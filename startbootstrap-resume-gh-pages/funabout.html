    This is a picture of my Family. I love the identity of having 4 sisters. It has been so fun to grow up with them. We each have a distinct personality so that makes it fun. As we have gotten older I think we look more and more like each other which is fun. Back home everyone calls us "The Girlers" instead of the Boyers. My Dad is a really good sport about it all. My parents are just amazing too. I'm so grateful for the intentional way they parented me.

    These are some of my friends in my major, Experience Design and Management. I have really enjoyed being creative and learning from them. It makes school just that much more enjoyable! This picture is us at our final experience testing. We gathered data, and designed an experience to test a time management app for BYU Marriott students. I hope to continue to design better experiences for others. I find the most fulfillment in seeing others happy.

    One of the greatest blessings of my life this past year has been working with missionaries for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. I served a mission in Chicago from Aug 2020- Feb 2022. It was one of the most rewarding things ever. I loved the people there. It has beeen so neat to give back and teach new missionaries. I have grown as an individual and have loved learning from these amazing and bright young people.

    A list of Riley's favorite scriptures

    1. 3 Nephi 9:13-14
      • I love the Savior's good example of always inviting in these verses
      • The blessings from these verses are wonderful; being healed, having eternal life, mercy extended to you, and being blessed
    2. Alma 26: 37
      • I love that this testifies of God's perfect mindfulness of each person
      • I recognize that God loves me through this verse
      • I have felt and seek after the ability to feel this love for other people
    3. 2 Nephi 4:16-28
      • These verses are so helpful in knowing that everyone has hard days, even Nephi
      • I know I can have gratitude in any circumstance because of these verses.
      • I love that when we look back it elevates our current faith. I want to always be increasing in faith.
